Hi there. I'm Laura

I'm a data journalist based in Barcelona.

I turn data into stories at La Vanguardia since 2015. During the first 5 years, I worked as the only data journalist in the newsroom. From October 2021 I coordinate a small data unit. I cover the whole process: gathering data, analyzing it, reporting, writing, and creating data visualizations. I love digging into any kind of data but specially if it can be drawn on a map. I've collaborated with data teams from other european countries to conduct cross border investigations.

I also teach data journalism as an associate professor at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and Blanquerna - Universitat Ramon Llull.
